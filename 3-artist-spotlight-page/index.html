About the Artist

Elena Vasquez is a renowned contemporary abstract artist whose vibrant works explore the intersection of color, emotion, and movement. Born in Barcelona in 1987, she developed her distinctive style through years of experimentation with mixed media and unconventional techniques.

Her work has been featured in galleries across Europe and North America, with collectors praising her ability to capture the essence of human experience through bold geometric forms and dynamic color palettes. Elena's pieces often incorporate elements of her Spanish heritage, creating a unique fusion of traditional and contemporary artistic expression.

Currently based in New York, Elena continues to push the boundaries of abstract art, constantly evolving her practice and inspiring a new generation of artists through her innovative approach to form and color.
